Who am I?

I am experienced with a wide variety of creative tools and technical skills and speak fluent English, Dutch and Russian. An anonymous secret-sharing website written in Python using the Django framework and an open source typing game written using the Arcade library.

Aside from programming, I also enjoy playing the guitar, playing the keyboard and producing music. Within my lifetime I have lived in multiple countries for a few years at a time, familiarizing myself with multiple cultures and traditions.
