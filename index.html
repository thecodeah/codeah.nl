Who am I?

Hello there! My name is Demian, also known as Codeah online. I'm originally from the Netherlands and am currently studying Computer Engineering in Warsaw, Poland. I speak fluent English, Dutch and Russian.

I have been interested in programming since the age of ten. Now, almost a decade later I am still heavily invested in computer programming. Some of my recent projects include: a Discord currency bot written in Go that is active on over one hundred servers, an anonymous secret-sharing website written in Python using the Django framework and an open source multiplayer video game mod.

Aside from programming, I also enjoy playing the guitar and travelling! You can find some of the songs I have produced on my SoundCloud!
